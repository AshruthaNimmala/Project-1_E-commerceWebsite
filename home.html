Welcome to Guardian Ride for SHE

We provide several services for women such as New Government schemes especially focusing on woman safety and help line numbers. You can access informative guides and tutorials. Your safety and comfort are our top priorities.

Today, many women suffer from domestic violence and are afraid to talk about it. According to international studies, this problem will be confronted not only in India, but also in many countries of Europe, America, and Canada. We offer this application that includes step-by-step instructions on how to act if you have been victimized by violence and several other features.

You can send us suggestions, tokens of appreciation, or criticism using the Contact Us page.

"Together, let's make safety a reality for all women."
